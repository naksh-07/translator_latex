NOTE ON THE TEXT (Boring *gyaan* shuru)
Suno, yeh *The Hobbit* pehli baar 1937 mein publish hua tha. Iske baad, 1951 mein Chapter V, “Riddles in the Dark,” ko *properly* revise kiya gaya. Kyunki OG version *Lord of the Rings* (Jo tab *in progress* thi) ki *Vibe* se match nahi kar raha tha. Author ko *continuity* ka *scene* banana tha!

Phir Tolkien uncle ne American edition (1966) aur British third edition mein bhi aur *further revisions* kiye. Matlab, kitni baar revise karoge yaar? OCD ka next level.

1995 mein, jab book ko pehli baar *word-processing files* (computer) mein daala gaya, tab pata chala ki kitne *misprints* aur errors the. *Librarians* ki party ho gayi hogi. Us din se lekar aaj tak, isi computerized text file se saari editions chhapi hain.

Aur ab, *Douglas A. Anderson* (May 2001) keh rahe hain ki inhone sab purane versions ko *line-by-line* check kiya hai taaki aapko *best, final, original* Tolkien *Vibe* mile. Thank you, bro. *Bohat mehnat lagi hogi.*

Author's Note (Linguistic *Gyaan*)

Yeh kahani *bohot* purane time ki hai. Tab languages aur letters ka *scene* alag tha. Humne English use ki hai, par do baatein note kar lo:

(1) English mein *dwarf* ka sirf ek hi correct plural hai: 'dwarfs', aur adjective hai 'dwarfish'. Lekin *hum* is book mein 'dwarves' aur 'dwarvish' use karenge. Haan, thoda *extra* hai, par inka *scene* alag hai. Yeh special spelling sirf Thorin Oakenshield aur uski *gang* ke liye hai. *Aise hi.*

(2) 'Orc' English word nahi hai. Yeh ek-do jagah aata hai, par generally isko 'goblin' kehte hain (ya *bade* walon ko 'hobgoblin'). 'Orc' toh hobbits ka *flex* hai, jo woh in creatures ko bulate the. Aur haan, iska humare sea-animal 'ork' se koi *connection* nahi hai. *Chill karo.*

Runes: Yeh woh purane letters the jo originally lakdi, pathar ya metal pe *scratch* kiye jaate the. Toh obviously, yeh patle aur *angular* hote the. Is kahani ke time sirf Dwarves hi inhein regular use karte the (mostly secret *chats* aur *private records* ke liye). Humne English Runes use kiye hain, jo aaj kisi ko nahi aate.

Agar Thror ke Map ke Runes dekho aur modern letters se compare karo, toh *alphabet* discover ho jayega. (Yeah, right. Kaun time waste karega?)

Basically, yeh sab signs diye hain—jaise *secret door* pe 'D' mark, aur woh *moon-runes* jo Elrond ne padhe the. Aur haan, Dwarves ke map mein East *upar* hota hai, *as usual* (like, *why*?). Clockwise padho: E(ast), S(outh), W(est), N(orth). *The End.*
